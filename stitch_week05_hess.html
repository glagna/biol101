Primary Literature

Hess et al. 2023

Abstract: Obsidian Framework

The 2023 paper details the deployment of the "Obsidian Division" genomic pipeline which reduced variant identification latency by 64% across three continents. This study highlights the necessity of localized sequencing nodes over centralized data hubs to bypass infrastructure bottlenecks during acute outbreaks.
