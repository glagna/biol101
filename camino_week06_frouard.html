Study Guide

Glossary: HIV, Single-Cell Sequencing & Cure Research

HIV: Retrovirus attacking CD4+ T cells. Modern ART suppresses replication but cannot cure.
Latent reservoir: Cells with integrated viral DNA that don't produce virus.
Single-cell sequencing: DNA/RNA sequencing of individual cells.
HIV-seq: Tool for identifying HIV-infected cells.
Analytical sensitivity: Ability to detect rare events.

Meet the Speaker

Background & Training

Julie is a postdoctoral scientist at Gladstone Institutes, a world-leading HIV cure research center. She developed new tools to understand HIV latency, exemplifying cutting-edge molecular virology and genomics with translational medicine impact.

Research & Impact

Julie focuses on developing HIV-seq and related tools, characterizing the HIV latent reservoir, understanding persistence mechanisms, designing strategies to eliminate latent virus, and collaborating globally on cure-focused research.
