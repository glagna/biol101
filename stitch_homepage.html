A Note on What This Course Is (and Isn't)

What It Is

A high-level intellectual sandbox. It is an exploration of the frontier. It is a place to ask "How?" and "Why?" before "What?".

What It Isn't

A standard memorization-based biology course. We do not focus on textbooks; we focus on the researchers writing the next chapter of human knowledge.
